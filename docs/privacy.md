# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

WinPrint ("the app") is a source-code and text **printing tool** published by
Kindel, LLC. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

## The short version

- WinPrint runs **locally** on your computer. The files you open and print are
  processed on your device. **Their contents are never uploaded to us or anyone else.**
- WinPrint does **not** require an account, show ads, or sell your data.

## Files you open and print

The documents you open, preview, and print are read from your device and rendered
locally so they can be printed. WinPrint does not transmit, store, or share the
contents of your files.

## Optional, anonymous diagnostics

WinPrint can collect **anonymous** usage and crash diagnostics to help fix bugs and
improve the app. This is **off by default** and can be turned off at any time.

- **Provider:** Microsoft Azure Application Insights.
- **What may be collected when enabled:** app version, operating system and version,
  non-identifying feature-usage events, and crash/exception details (stack traces).
- **What is never collected:** the contents or names of your files, your identity,
  or any personal information.

## Update checks and downloads

When you download or update WinPrint (for example via GitHub Releases, winget, or
Homebrew — with app-store distribution such as the Microsoft Store or Mac App Store
planned for the future), those services operate under their own privacy policies and
may receive standard network information such as your IP address as an inherent part
of serving the download.

## Third parties

WinPrint relies on: Microsoft Azure Application Insights (optional diagnostics, above),
and your chosen distribution channel (GitHub Releases, winget, or Homebrew today;
Microsoft Store and Apple App Store planned) for installation and updates.

## Children

WinPrint is a general-purpose developer/productivity tool and is not directed at
children.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
date above.
